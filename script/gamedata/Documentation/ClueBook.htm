-Quests from the Wizards

After Zamora has been struck down in the Great Hall, you will be instructed by
the governors to trek forth and return the orb. To begin your journey, you must
first obtain Zamora's journal from the bookshelf in his room on the second floor
of the Athaneum. Unfortunately for you, the journal is locked. The key can be
purchased at the general supplies shop in the Thieves Guild. Upon using the key
and reading the first page of the journal, you will be instructed to seek out
"the hermit".

Flagell

Flagell is called "the hermit". He lives at (B in Mine 3) a cave to the west of the
waterfall. [Saying "Journal" to the wizards will cause them to read it.] After
reading the journal, Flagell will send you to kill a beast (a wyvern), that lives to
the north of his cave, known for terrorizing miners. After you have slain the
wyvern, Flagell will grant to you a Scroll. Following the wizard's last words, an
apparition of the orb will appear. The faint voice of Zamora will then say...

"The first of last is third, and the third of first is last."

The solution to this riddle is, "_ _ L _ _ _ _ R ".

Return to top

Paundor

After reading a page from the journal "the diplomat", who can be found in
Port Hope, will send you to a man named Ian. Ian can be found in the Mantov
tavern. He will tell you that he recently sold a special weapon at the town of
Moloch. The grapnel arrow can be purchased at the Moloch general supplies
shop. Upon returning to Paundor with the arrow, he will tell you to find and
retrieve the great red gem, which is located in the caves to the north (Mine 26\27)
Once you have found the red gem, use the grapnel arrow to pull it from the cave
wall. (Be sure and have some rope) Now Paundor will be able to grind the gem
into a fine glowing red powder, which he will give you. After speaking with
Paundor an apparition of the orb will appear and the voice of Zamora will say...

"Half of 'W' is sixth."

The solution to this riddle is, " _ _ _ _ _ V _ _ ".

Return to top

Bysette

This kind old wizard that dwells in the cleric town of Moloch, is the one known
as the "lover of nature". Upon reading Zamora's journal, he will ask you to return
a ring that was lost aboard the pirate ship Blackmane. The Captain of this vessel
can be found in the tavern at Helsingor. The pirate's name is Captain Chigon.
After defeating the evil Chigon, you will find Bysette's ring in his chest. Bysette
will now exchange a magical branch for his ring. Upon leaving Bysette, an
apparition of the orb will appear. You will then hear the voice of Zamora say...

"It is in the middle backwards."

The solution to this riddle is, "_ _ _ T I _ _ _".

Return to top

Prezlin

"The merchant", who can be found first in Duomin, will read the journal and send
you to check on the well being of Winze. Winze's wife, Joan, can be found in the
town of Devon next to her late husbands grave. You travel now to the
New Devon alchemist to give Prezlin the bad news, that Winze is dead. Prezlin
will now give you a vial of magic liquid from his shop. After talking with Prezlin
an apparition of the orb will appear and the voice of Zamora will say...

"One in from each end is 'A'."

The solution to this riddle is, "_ A_ _ _ _ A _".

Return to top

Paltivar

Paltivar can first be found in Devon. After looking at Zamora's journal he will send
you to find a man named Joseph. Joseph can be found in the Mantov jail. He will
ask you to get a bottle of sweet wine for him. The bottle can be bought at the
tavern in Stachus. After giving the bottle to Joseph, he will elude to some sort of a
password.

Return to top

Griffin

This wizard is known as "the scholar". Griffin can be found on the second level of
the Athaneum. Before he will translate his section of the journal, he will ask you who
the author of "Spellcasting" is. By picking up books in the library bookcases, you
will eventually find that Griffin himself is the author of the book. Upon relaying your
discovery to him he will send you to retrieve the hourglass of stopped time, which is
guarded by two stoned dragons in the dessert. He then tells you that his assistant
Yarte was looking for an entrance to the dessert near the Stone Giant town. While
talking to Yarte in the giant prison, he will give you map A5, which shows the
entrance into the dessert from Mine 6. After traveling through Mine 5 and battling
the Paleoscincus, you will eventually end up at the northern end of the dessert.
Traveling almost due south you will find the hourglass, as well as the two stoned
dragons. Fighting the two dragons is the only way to retrieve the hourglass. When
you have returned the hourglass to Griffin, he will break it open and give you some
of its sand. When you have received the sand from him, an apparition of the orb
will appear. The ghostly voice of Zamora will then say...

"He and Prezlin begin the same."

The solution to this riddle is, "P_ _ _ _ _ _ _".

Return to top

Winze

Although Winze is dead you can give Gabriel's flower to Joan at Winze's grave in
Devon. You can buy the flower from Gabriel in Mantov. After Joan has received the
flower, Winze's ghost will appear. Upon talking to him, he will send you to the
underworld through the portal on the northern wall of the graveyard to get a small
amount of lava. Once you have defeated Demonacus you must take his Amulet of
lava and return to Winze. After talking with the late wizard an apparition of the orb
will appear, you will then hear the voice of Zamora say...

"Eight letters make up his name."

In order to solve this riddle you must first have completed all other wizard's quests,
w ith the exception of Quai.

By putting all answers together you will know that "PALTIVAR" is the answer.

Return to top

Quai and the Final Battle

"The explorer", Quai, will tell you to slay the beast which is keeping him prisoner
here. The Cynotaur can be found at E in Mine 35. Upon killing this creature you
must pick up his horn from the chest and take it to Quai. The skilled alchemist will
now take all of your special items and mix them together in the horn to form the
horn of encasement. He will only do this if you've have completed all of the other
wizards quests. Now you are ready to work your way through the caves and find
the entrance to the Castle of imprisonment. (B in Mine 34) By responding to the
device just in front of the trap door and typing, "PALTIVAR", which is the
collective solution to the orb riddles, it will disappear and you will be able to enter
the castle. After fighting your way up to the fifth level of the castle you will discover
that Paltivar was the one who had stolen the orb from Zamora in the Great Hall.
After beating Paltivar to within two health points of death, the wizards will instruct
you to use the horn on the orb to defuse any magical hold that Paltivar might have
on it, and then bring it with you on your return to Zamora's side.The portal past the
altar will take you to the Great Hall.

Return to top

-Other Tasks

Duomin

Upon talking to Whitney in Duomin, you will soon discover that her son has been
taken by a red dragon. In order to rescue Whitney's son from the dragon you must
enter Mine 11. As soon as you enter the cave you will find yourself face to face with
the red dragon. After you have defeated him, Whitney's son will run home to his mother
in Duomin.

Return to top

Helsingor

The woman named Olga (location 7 in Helsingor) will tell you that a priceless piece of
art work has been stolen from her home. Next you must proceed to a place where
stolen items would most likely be found, the thieves guild. You will find the work of art
hanging on a wall at location 5 in the guild. Simply pick up the item, return it to Olga and
you will be rewarded handsomely.

Return to top

Moloch

Upon visiting Moloch, an interesting person to speak with would be Vance. This man will
ask you to get rid of the undeads in the graveyard at the northern tip of town. After
completing this task, you must talk with either Gamal (if you have not donated to the
healers), or Cedric (if you have donated to the healers). Gamal will give you a gold reward
of 500, or a holy reward of 25 to your current HP. Cedric will give you a gold reward of
5,000, or 25 to your maximum HP.

Return to top

New Devon

By talking with the governor of New Devon, you will discover that he is preparing a peace
treaty that must be signed by the giant king. If you choose to deliver the treaty, the
governor will grant to your party three two-handed swords +2. (The giant king can always
be found sitting upon his throne at location 1 in the stone giant's town.) After attempting to
deliver the treaty you will learn that the king needs the giant glass of magnification to read
the tiny writing. The glass can be found in a buried chest to the west of the waterfall. After
returning the signed treaty to the governor, the number of giants near New Devon will
decrease.

Return to top

Port Hope

The governor of Port Hope would like for you to return the amulet of Anatolay, which has
been buried in a chest (location 1 in the catacombs under Anatolay) for many years. Once
you have found the chest, you must open it with care, for the electric shock trap could
cause quite a bit of damage to your party.

Return to top

Saccate

In the early stages of the game, the only task to be performed, which you learn from the
governor of Saccate, is to battle the monsters in and around the various mines in the north
western part of the island.

Return to top

Stachus

Several people will tell you that a healing stone has been stolen from a pedestal in the center
of Stachus by a group of mine trolls. These mine trolls can be found at B in Mine 22. After
obtaining the healing stone, you can return it to Murray in Stachus, who will be glad to
reward you.

Return to top
